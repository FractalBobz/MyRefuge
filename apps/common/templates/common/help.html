Help

MyRefuge is the AirBnB for refugees.

You can currently register as a refuge provider (an awesome person who wants to share their home with a refugee) and as a refugee. Soon, refugees will be able to contact refuge providers directly and request to stay in their home.

To ensure that this works well we are currently working on

  • Identity authentication
  • Refugee status confirmation
  • Integration of NGOs so that they can support refuge seekers and refuge providers forming relationships
If you have any questions or suggestions about MyRefuge, or if you'd like to work with us, please message us on https://www.facebook.com/MyRefugeApp
